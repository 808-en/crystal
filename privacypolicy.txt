PRIVACY POLICY

Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website and submit an application or survey response. By using the site or submitting information, you agree to the practices described here.

Data We Collect
When you submit an application form, we store only the information you voluntarily provide, including:

Your Bloxd.io username
Your BloxdHub @handle
Your requested role
Additional application details you include in text fields
Possibly your CipherChat (cipherchat-theta.base44.app), if you include it in your application
During the survey portion, we may also collect your state/region and country based on your approximate location.

Approximate Location (Survey)
If you participate in our survey, we may collect your approximate state/region and country. This is used to understand respondent location for our survey purposes. We do not collect or store your IP address in this process.

How We Use Your Information
We use the information you provide to:

Review and process your application
Understand survey response location (state/region and country)
Contact you regarding your application (if applicable)
No Endorsement / No Affiliation
We are not endorsed by, affiliated with, or sponsored by Bloxd LLC.

Not Affiliated With Formspree
We are not affiliated with Formspree.io.

We Do Not Sell Your Information
We do not sell your personal information.

Data Storage
We store only the information submitted through our application form, plus the survey location details (state/region and country), as described above.

Security
We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect the information you provide against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Contact
